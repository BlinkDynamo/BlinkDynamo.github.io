Hi! My name is Josh Hayden. Online I go by 'BlinkDynamo', often 'Blink' for short. I'm obsessed with programming and computers, focussing most of my energy on system tools and low-level software. My high-level software projects are often video games or .NET apps.

My Core Values

Tech is often driven by money somewhere along the way. I understand the neccessity of this for cultivating innovation and new ideas. That being said, all of the greats in software development that I look up to have done what they've done for the core reason of solving extremely difficult problems for the everyday programmer years later, free of charge. Here are some names that come to mind:

  • Linus Torvalds - Creator of the Linux kernel.
  • Ken Thompson - Creator of B, Co-creator of UNIX and UTF-8 encoding.
  • Dennis Ritchie - Creator of C, Co-creator of UNIX.
  • Richard Stallman - Founder of the GNU Project and developer of countless GNU softwares (GCC, glibc, GNU Bash, coreutils, Emacs, etc.).
  • Ramon Santamaria - Creator of raylib, along with countless educational tools for beginners.
